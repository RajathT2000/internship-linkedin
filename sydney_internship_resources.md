## Tips for Finding Internships

1. **Set up job alerts** on Seek, Indeed, LinkedIn for "AI Internship Sydney"
2. **Follow companies** on LinkedIn to see job postings
3. **Attend career fairs** at Macquarie University
4. **Join MQ PACE** networking events
5. **Connect with alumni** working in AI/ML roles
6. **Apply directly** on company websites
7. **Network on LinkedIn** with recruiters and hiring managers
8. **Join tech communities** - Sydney Python, Data Science meetups
9. **Check government programs** - NSW GovTECH internships
10. **Consider startups** - Often more flexible with internships

## Best Times to Apply

- **July-August**: Applications for November/December summer internships
- **November-January**: Summer internship season
- **February-March**: Mid-year internship applications
- **Ongoing**: Many companies accept applications year-round
